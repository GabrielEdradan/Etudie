Frequently Asked Questions

About the service

How does Étudie work?

Étudie uses state-of-the-art image processing and computer vision technology, leveraging recent advancements in AI and deep learning. It performs custom image operations on the answer sheets and sends them to our own in-house image classifier.

What tests can I use Étudie on?

Currently, Étudie can be used on 50-item tests with choices from A to D. Future versions will have more customization options.

Is it compatible with different types of paper?

Currently, Étudie should only be used for standard yellow notepads. Future versions will accommodate different paper formats.

What are the paper specifications when using Étudie?

The answer sheet must have the header (the student information) one line apart from the content (the student answers). The answers must be in two columns, and the item answers must be substantially spaced from the item numbers.

What analytics does Étudie offer?

Currently, Étudie only offers score and item analytics, as well as the answer sheets with annotated correct answers. Future versions will perform more analysis.

How secure is the data uploaded to Étudie?

All user data is protected with end-to-end encryption. We do not share your data with third parties.

About the service

How do I pay for my plan?

You can pay for your plan securely through our online payment portal using a credit card or debit card. We accept all major credit cards.

Are there any additional fees beyond the subscription cost?

No. Your monthly subscription fee covers all the features and functionality of your chosen plan.

When and how can I cancel my plan?

You can cancel your plan at any time directly from your account dashboard. There are no cancellation fees, and you will have access to your plan for the remainder of your billing period.

Can I get a refund for a canceled plan?

We are confident in the quality of our service, but we do consider refunds for reasonable claims and causes of dissatisfaction. Don’t hesitate to reach out when you have questions or when you encounter any issues.
